What is .mp4?

MP4 is short for MPEG-4 (.mp4) video file, which is a compressed video file format that can hold video, audio and subtitles. You will typically see .mp4 files when you download a video from the internet. The easiest way to open these files is to simply double-click on them and your computer will open the file with the appropriate program. These files (.mp4) were created to help software and hardware developers create media that was flexible and adaptable to technologies like digital television, animation graphics, internet, and other uses. Today, .mp4 files are still widely used and are compatible for playback on most video players, including iOS and Android devices. You can stream .mp4 files effortlessly in their compressed format on your computer, phone or tablet.
