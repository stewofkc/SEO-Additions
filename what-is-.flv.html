What is .flv?

.flv are Flash Video files. Flash Video files use Adobe Flash Player to send video or audio over the internet. Flash Video was the standard video format for years, however, most websites have moved to HTML5. Flash Video (.flv) files typically contain interleaved digital video and audio data. If you want to open a .flv file, you'll need a media player that's compatible with the file type. You can use Windows Media Player or VLC media player to open .flv files on your computer.
